BOX SHOOTER

October 2020

Box Shooter is a First Person Shooter game where you shoots boxes to score points. There is a timer that decreases when you shoots a white box and increases when you shoot yellow boxes. You score points when you shoot green boxes. The game's difficulty gradually increases as the spawning rate of the white boxes increases over time.
